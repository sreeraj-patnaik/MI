The Captain


Hardik Pandya, an all-rounder renowned for his dynamic performances, has been a pivotal figure in the IPL. He began his journey with the Mumbai Indians (MI) in 2015, contributing significantly to their multiple title wins. In 2022, he led Gujarat Titans (GT) to a title victory in their debut season. In 2024, MI made a bold decision, appointing him as their captain, succeeding Rohit Sharma.

The Leader


Rohit Sharma's legacy with Mumbai Indians is unparalleled. He took over as captain in 2013, guiding MI to five IPL titles and cementing their dominance. Though MI transitioned leadership to Hardik Pandya in 2024, Rohit's presence remains invaluable. His mentorship and strategic acumen continue to be crucial in shaping the team's future.
